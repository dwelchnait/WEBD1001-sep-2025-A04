Steps to make mash potatoes

  1. Pick 3 - 4 white potatoes
  2. Peel potatoes and cut in quarters
  3. Turn on stove burner to medium
  4. Place potatoes in pot and cover with cold water
  5. Place on stove burner
  6. Boil until potatoes can be easily pieced with a fork
  7. Remove and drain water
  8. with a potato masher, mash potatoes to a desirable consistency
