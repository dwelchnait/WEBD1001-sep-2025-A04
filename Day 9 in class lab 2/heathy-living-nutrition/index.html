How to make Healthy Protein Bars

For ingredients, you can use rolled oats, protein powder, peanut butter or any nut butter, honey or agave, flex seed, banana for flavour and binding, and optional dates, dried fruit, chocolate chips or coconut flakes. Then follow the steps below:

  1. Mix all ingredients until well combined
  2. Press firmly into an 8x8 pan
  3. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour
  4. Cut and store bars in the fridge for up to 2 weeks
