Drama

Overwhelmed by the choices available when it comes to picking a drama movie? You're not alone.

The drama genre in movies focuses on realistic storytelling, character-driven narratives, and emotional depth. These films often explore serious themes such as love, conflict, morality, relationships, personal struggles, and social issues. The goal is to evoke strong emotions and connect the audience to the characters' experiences.

Top 3 Drama Recommendations

No matter what kind of drama you're in the mood for, the recommendations ahead have something for every type of moviegoer. Find your next watch in our top three picks for the greatest drama movies of all time.

Forrest Gump (1994)

The history of the United States from the 1950s to the '70s unfolds from the perspective of an Alabama man with an IQ of 75, who yearns to be reunited with his childhood sweetheart.

Schindler's List (1993)

In German-occupied Poland during World War II, industrialist Oskar Schindler gradually becomes concerned for his Jewish workforce after witnessing their persecution by the Nazis.

Casablanca (1942)

A cynical expatriate American cafe owner struggles to decide whether or not to help his former lover and her fugitive husband escape the Nazis in French Morocco.
