Action

The action genre is all about high-energy storytelling, fast-paced sequences, and adrenaline-pumping excitement. These movies often feature intense fight scenes, explosive stunts, thrilling chases, and heroic protagonists taking on dangerous challenges.

The top Action Movies from the 80s, 90s, and 2000s

Action movies from the 1980s, 1990s, and 2000s helped shape the genre, introducing legendary heroes, groundbreaking stunts, and iconic one-liners. Here's a look at some of the best action films from each decade:

Die Hard (1988)

A New York City police officer tries to save his estranged wife and several others taken hostage by terrorists during a Christmas party at the Nakatomi Plaza in Los Angeles.

The Matrix (1999)

When a beautiful stranger leads computer hacker Neo to a forbidding underworld, he discovers the shocking truth--the life he knows is the elaborate deception of an evil cyber-intelligence.

John Wick(2014)

John Wick is a former hitman grieving the loss of his true love. When his home is broken into, robbed, and his dog killed, he is forced to return to action to exact revenge.
