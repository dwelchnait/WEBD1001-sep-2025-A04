Steps for making Sourdough Bread

  1. Mix the dough
  2. Let it rise
  3. Shape the dough
  4. Proofing (resting)
  5. Bake it

Baking Terms

Fermentation
The process where yeast eats sugars from flour and releases carbon dioxide, causing the dough to rise.
Kneading
Working the dough to develop gluten and create a structure.
Proofing
The second rise of the shaped dough before baking.
